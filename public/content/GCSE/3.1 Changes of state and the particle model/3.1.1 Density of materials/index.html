What is density?

Density is the ratio of mass to volume.

Objects with the greater mass but which take up the same volume are more/less dense. Objects with the same mass but which take up more volume are more/less dense.

Will ice float?

Predict-explain-observe-explain

  • Write a prediction.
    • ice will float/not float.
  • Write an explanation.
    • why do you think this?
  • Write an observation.
    • ice does/does not float.
  • Write an explanation.
    • were you right? Explain.
